Biography

2011, the year I got my first guitar after months of begging to my parents. I remember listening to my neighbor play Dukkho Bilash | দুঃখ বিলাশ through my window. I spent all night listening to his music and thinking ‘What a rockstar!’ I used to daydream that I would play an instrument on a big stage. That dream still holds. I’m still working on that dream.
