Biography

A 21 years old musician from Bangladesh, striving to get his music heard across the world; From writing to composing and playing instrument then recording, Jamil Jahangir does it all, as a solo artist.

With his material like “Dive”, Jamil Jahangir has shown that he can put out a well written music that is versatile and captivating.

A 21 years old musician from Bangladesh, striving to get his music heard across the world; From writing to composing and playing instrument then recording, Jamil Jahangir does it all, as a solo artist.

With his material like “Dive”, Jamil Jahangir has shown that he can put out a well written music that is versatile and captivating.



2011, the year I got my first guitar after months of begging to my parents. I remember listening to my neighbor play Dukkho Bilash | দুঃখ বিলাশ through my window. I spent all night listening to his music and thinking ‘What a rockstar!’ I used to daydream that I would play an instrument on a big stage. That dream still holds. I’m still working on that dream. That is my I’ve created jamilmusic.cf, to connect with you guys, share my music and spread love.
